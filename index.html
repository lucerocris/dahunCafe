About Us

In the bustling streets of Cebu, Dahun (Cebuano for 'leaf') stands as a verdant sanctuary where exceptional coffee meets island hospitality. Our café brings together locally-sourced beans, thoughtfully crafted dishes, and a cozy tropical atmosphere that makes every visit feel like a warm embrace from nature itself.
